Why it Works

Over-drinking Explained

In short, dopamine is a neurotransmitter in charge of making us feel short-term pleasure. Alcohol increases the production of dopamine, making your brain want more. Unfortunately, alcohol simultaneously hinders other parts of your brain in charge of decision-making, speech, and coordination. That’s why it can often feel like it’s hard to stop once you start.

Our Approach

BuzzSmart uses a friendly form of Aversive Conditioning , a behavioral therapy that involves repeat pairing an unwanted behavior with discomfort. We are not fans of discomfort here at BuzzSmart, so instead we give you a gentle nudge when you are out drinking in the form of a smart notification, reminding you that to pace yourself and stay hydrated.

You are in control

The power is in your hands, quite literally! We'll get to know your habits and offer personalized suggestions, but ultimately, it's up to you to decide if you want to embrace them. We'll send you friendly notifications, and hey, you can switch them off anytime you like. Since you've come this far, it looks like you're ready to embark on a positive journey of change!
